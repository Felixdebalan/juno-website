Common Questions

Everything about Perpetual Concierge

Answers to the most common questions from New Jersey business owners considering an AI phone answering service.

What exactly does Perpetual Concierge do?+

It deploys a custom AI voice agent that handles every inbound call 24/7 — with zero human involvement in the qualification loop. Every call goes through triple qualification: voice intake (what they have, contact info, intent), mid-call SMS photo collection (photos arrive before you call back), and location confirmation (verifies proximity before any commitment). You receive a complete lead summary with photos before you ever pick up the phone.

How much does it cost?+

Pricing is customized based on your business size and call volume. There's a one-time setup fee and a monthly retainer — both discussed during your private consultation. The setup fee is deferred until the agent is live and you've confirmed it works. You don't pay for something that isn't running.

Will my customers know they're talking to an AI?+

Most callers never know. The agent speaks naturally, asks normal questions, and moves the conversation forward the same way a real person would. We have 85 consecutive calls handled for a live client with zero complaints. If a caller directly asks, the agent can be configured to acknowledge it — that's your call.

My calls are too complex for AI.+

The agent doesn't replace your judgment — it replaces the intake step. It captures, qualifies, and requests photos so that when you do get involved, you already have what you need to decide. You handle the complexity. The agent handles the phone. It also supports live transfers to you during business hours for callers who need a human.

What if the AI makes a mistake?+

The agent's job is to capture and qualify — never to quote, commit, or promise anything on your behalf. If it misunderstands something, the lead still gets captured and you still get the callback. The floor is a voicemail with a photo. The ceiling is a fully pre-qualified appointment.

I already have voicemail. Why do I need this?+

Voicemail captures a message. It doesn't qualify the lead, ask for photos, or confirm location. 78% of customers buy from whoever responds first — if they hit voicemail, they're already calling your competitor. The agent answers on the first ring, every time. NJ Antiques handled 85 calls in 8 days: zero missed, 74 selling leads.

How long does setup take?+

Most clients go live within 14 days. We start with an onboarding form to capture your business details, then build and test the agent before handoff. No new hardware, apps, or technical knowledge required. Your existing business number forwards to the agent — that's it.

Do I still need to answer calls?+

No. Juno handles the phone so you stay focused on the job in front of you. Every lead is qualified and summarized before you call back — no more stopping work to take notes, no more callbacks with zero context.
